Chocolate Cake

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just a sweet treat. Made with cocoa powder, butter, and a touch of coffee for depth of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 & 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
