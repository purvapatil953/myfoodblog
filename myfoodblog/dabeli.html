Kutchi Dabeli

Dabeli is a popular street food from Gujarat made with spicy mashed potatoes, chutneys, and roasted peanuts, stuffed in pav bread and served with sev and pomegranate seeds.

Ingredients:

  • 4 pav buns
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 2 tbsp dabeli masala
  • 2 tbsp tamarind chutney
  • 2 tbsp garlic chutney
  • 1/4 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 2 tbsp roasted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup sev
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan, add mashed potatoes, dabeli masala, and salt. Mix well.
  2. Cut pav buns and toast them with butter.
  3. Spread garlic chutney on one side and tamarind chutney on the other.
  4. Fill with potato mixture, peanuts, pomegranate seeds, and sev.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy.
