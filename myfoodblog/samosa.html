Crispy Samosa

Samosa is a crispy and savory pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas. It's a perfect snack for tea time and a popular street food in India.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil to form a crumbly texture. Add water and knead into a stiff dough. Set aside for 30 mins.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, mashed potatoes, peas, and spices. Mix well and let it cool.
  3. Roll the dough into circles, cut in half, form cones, and fill with potato mixture.
  4. Seal edges with water and deep fry until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with chutney.
